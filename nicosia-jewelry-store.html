Jewelry Store in Galaxias Shopping Centre

Our Nicosia Makarios jewellery store occupies a location within Galaxias Shopping Centre, bringing traditional Cypriot goldsmithing into a contemporary retail setting. Every piece of jewelry originates from our workshop, crafted using techniques that have defined Mediterranean gold work for millennia.

This location serves those seeking the convenience of modern shopping combined with access to authentic heritage jewelry. Our store provides an opportunity to discover jewelry designed to last—rings, earrings, necklaces, and bracelets that reference Cyprus's history from Neolithic times through Byzantine periods.

Centrally located with ample parking and extended hours, this jewelry store makes traditional craftsmanship accessible. We maintain rigorous standards: solid 18-karat gold, hand-hammered construction, historically-informed designs, and attention to detail that distinguishes craft from manufacturing.
