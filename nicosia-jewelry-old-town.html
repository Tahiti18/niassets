Historic Jewelry Store in Nicosia

Our Nicosia Old Town jewelry store is located on Onasagorou Street, within walking distance of the Cyprus Museum and Famagusta Gate. This jewellery shop serves as a destination for those seeking handcrafted gold jewelry rooted in Mediterranean tradition.

The pieces you'll find here—handcrafted from 18-karat gold—draw inspiration from the island's heritage: Neolithic simplicity, Bronze Age design, Byzantine beauty, and early Mediterranean forms. Each piece is crafted using traditional goldsmithing techniques passed down through generations.

Our Old Town location offers rings, earrings, necklaces, and bracelets designed to last a lifetime. Each piece is handcrafted in our Cyprus workshop using methods that predate written language on the island.
