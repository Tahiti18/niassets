About Our Limassol Jewellery Shop

Our Limassol jewelry store serves as a destination for those seeking authentic handcrafted gold jewelry in Cyprus. Located in Cyprus's second-largest city, we preserve traditional goldsmithing techniques including hand-hammering, granulation, and gold sheet work.

Each piece begins as solid 18-karat gold sheet, specially blended to achieve the warm yellow tone characteristic of Mediterranean antiquity. The jewelry you'll find here is crafted by hand—formed one strike of the hammer at a time, using methods unchanged for millennia.

Our Limassol location features rings inspired by Mycenaean sphinxes, earrings echoing Byzantine mosaics, and bracelets carrying island history. When you visit our jewelry store, you'll find pieces designed to be passed down through generations.
